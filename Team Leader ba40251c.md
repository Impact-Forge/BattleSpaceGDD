# Character Description

Helps the Squad Leader prepare their soldiers for wartime missions. This position supervises and manages other soldiers, also known as team leaders, within their squad. The team leader is the tactical center of a squad, maneuvering and leading their team to meet the objectives of the Squad Leaders.

# Special Abilities

They control the tempo and the pace of the fight, how they interact with their Soldiers. Direct comms to the Squad Leader or Commander. They are considered one of the most important jobs. Team Leaders serve as the first line leaders, they are the eyes and ears of the Squad Leader.
