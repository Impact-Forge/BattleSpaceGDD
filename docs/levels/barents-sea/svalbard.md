# Synopsis

You team will need to gain possession or control all key locations across the entire map, while simultaneously clearing and securing each strategic location so that you can build crucial infrastructure that allows you to maintain communication, resources and continuity of territory seized. &#x20;

# Level walkthrough

Players side will start south region located on Svalbard and is one of 16 main sectors . &#x20;

You have just entered the country Norway with the help of NATO with minimum equipment.  As the assaulting team you will have to move your coalition of foreign fighters north through each 16 sectors . As you seize additional territories throughout the region, your coalition forces will unlock additional assets that will help you achieve dominants throughout the region if utilized correctly.
