# Location(s)

### Odessa is a port city on the Black Sea and is economically crucial to Ukraine. It’s known for its beaches. It handled more than 70% of Ukraine's exports before the war.

**Area:** 62.71 mi²

# Level walkthrough

Player will start Reni Raion in region of  locatiated in the South Westen area of Odessa Oblast and is one of 7 main sectors . &#x20;

You have just entered the country Ukraine from Romanian with the help of NATO with minimum equipment.  As the assaulting team you will have to move your coalition of foreign fighters north through each 7 sectors . As you seize additional territories throughout the region, your coalition forces will unlock additional assets that will help you achieve dominants throughout the region if utilized correctly.
