As a Combat Medic, you'll administer emergency medical care in the field in combat, as well as primary care and health protection and evacuation from a point of injury.

# Special Abilities

Giving the situation allows it players will be able to give first aid to injured soldiers allowing them to get back into the fight. Medics can also revive some incompastated players unless they have bleeded out or been shot in the head.  Medics can also build forward MASH on the battlefield as long as they are near medical vehicle. This allows players to heal to 100% health.&#x20;

# Disadvantages:

Requires the player to have a high level of multitasking and attention his surroundings. Players will risk their life to get a teammate up. Medics can only heal injurys to 85% on the battlefield.&#x20;
