# Character Description

A combat engineer is a type of soldier who performs military engineering tasks in support of land forces combat operations. Combat engineers perform a variety of military engineering, and mine warfare tasks as well as construction and demolition duties in and out of combat zones.

# Special Abilities

You specialize in conducting raids and ***assault*** missions on enemy territory and you are the key element for setting up required key infrastructures of captured areas. Your capabilities include conducting anti- tank mining and explosive ambush missions, building key strategic facilities, and killing enemies. You also have a Tool Set that allows you to pick locks of non-destroyed enemy vehicles. These vehicles can be used but never rearmed or that can be brought back to the Motor Pool to cash it in for resource income.

# Disadvantages:

Requires the player to have a high level of multitasking and attention to the progression of battle as a whole on the map. Must set up crucial infrastructure of  captured area in order to bring it up on the grid.
