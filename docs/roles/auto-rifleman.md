# Character Description

The role of the ***Automatic Rifleman*** (AR) is to lay down a high volume of suppressive fire against area targets. He is a vital member of the fire team provides them with a mobile base of rapid fire when assaulting. When used in defensive operations, the Automatic Rifleman can lay down fires equivalent to multiple standard riflemen.\
